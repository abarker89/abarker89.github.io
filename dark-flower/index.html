Pacific Rhododendron

  • Common alternate names: Coast Rhododendron, California Rhododendron, California Rosebay, Big Leaf Rhododendron
  • Physical size:
    • Evergreen shrub or small tree
    • Typical height 3–15 ft (1–5 m)
    • Up to ~25 ft (7–8 m) in ideal conditions
  • Geographic range:
    • Western North America
    • British Columbia to central California
    • Common west of the Cascade Range
  • Best climates:
    • Cool, moist, temperate climates
    • Forests and montane areas
    • Acidic, well-draining soils
    • Often under Douglas-fir and hemlock
    • Blooms late spring to early summer
