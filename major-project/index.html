Welcome

This site is a fan-made exploration of Angel’s Egg, a surreal and visually striking animated film released in 1985. The movie is known for its minimal dialogue, emotional silence, and painterly imagery. Here, you can explore detailed character profiles, thematic analysis, production history, and the artistic influences behind this cult classic.

Whether you're discovering Angel’s Egg for the first time or revisiting it through a new lens, this site aims to highlight the film’s unique visual language, its symbolic storytelling, and the lasting creative impact it continues to have.
